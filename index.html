LUDO GAME

READY TO PLAY? Push START.
ROLL THE DICE TO PLAY.
RULES FOR THE GAME:

1. Each player rolls the die; the highest roller begins the game.
2. Players alternate turns in a clockwise direction.
3. To enter a token into play from its yard to its starting square, a player must roll a 6.
4. The player can draw a token from home every time he gets a 6 unless home is empty or move a piece 6 times.
5. If the player has no tokens yet in play and rolls other than a 6, the turn passes to the next player.
6. Passes are not allowed; if no move is possible, the turn moves to the next player.
7. If the player cannot draw a token from home, rolling a 6 earns the player an additional or "bonus" roll in that turn.
8. If the bonus roll results in a 6 again, the player earns again an additional bonus roll.If the third roll is also a 6, the player may not move and the turn immediately passes to the next player.
